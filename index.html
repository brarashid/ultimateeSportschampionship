About The Tournament

Built For Africa's Elite

A premier competitive gaming platform that celebrates skill, strategy, and the spirit of champions across the continent.

What Is UEC 2026?

The Ultimate eSport Championship is Africa's most prestigious gaming tournament, bringing together the continent's finest players across four flagship titles. Founded in 2020, UEC has grown into a multi-city event drawing thousands of spectators and hundreds of professional players.

This year, Accra, Ghana hosts the grand finals — a culmination of months of qualifiers, regional battles, and relentless competition. UEC 2026 represents not just a tournament, but a movement to position Africa at the global esports stage.

Our Mission

To discover, nurture, and celebrate African gaming talent by providing a world-class competitive platform with fair rules, transparent judging, and real financial rewards.

Our Vision

To become the foremost esports institution in Africa — producing players who compete on the global stage and inspiring the next generation of digital athletes.

Organized by POWERPLAY NETWORK.

Official organizer — Est. 2024, Accra, Ghana

Tournament Format

01

Group Stage

150+ teams divided into groups of 8. Each team plays round-robin matches. Top 2 from each group advance to knockouts.

02

Knockout Stage

Single-elimination bracket with best-of-3 series. Losers are eliminated; winners advance toward the grand final.

03

Grand Finals

Best-of-5 showdown on the main stage in Accra. Live audience, full broadcast, and ₵50,000 in prizes awarded.

Rules Summary

  • Players must be 16+ and present valid ID during registration
  • All matches are supervised by certified referees
  • Zero tolerance for cheating, hacks, or unsportsmanlike conduct
  • Players must check in 30 minutes before their scheduled match
  • Disconnections handled per game-specific protocols
  • Prize money disbursed within 90 days after end of the Tournament
